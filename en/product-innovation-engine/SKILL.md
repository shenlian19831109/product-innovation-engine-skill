# Needs Discovery & Product Innovation Engine (v9.0)

This skill provides a comprehensive guide from scenario input to product innovation solutions, aiming to transform fragmented user expressions into high-value product insights.

## Core Methodologies

1.  **Scenario Segmentation**: Breaking down broad scenarios into specific sub-scenarios.
2.  **Three-Layer Discovery**: Needs → Pain Points → Delight Points.
3.  **9 Major Needs Theories**: Deep diagnosis using Maslow, Two-Factor, Achievement Motivation, etc.
4.  **5 Whys**: Deep diving from pain points to root causes and deep delight points.
5.  **1-6 Value Ladder**: From basic functions (Survival) to disruptive functions (Brand).

## Execution Workflow

### 1. Scenario Segmentation
Deconstruct industry scenarios (e.g., E-commerce, Education, Office, Content Creation, Healthcare, Finance, Manufacturing) into specific process steps.
- **Action**: Identify industry workflows, supplement with unique scenarios, and ask the user to confirm the focused sub-scenario.

### 2. Data Collection & Organization
Collect user complaints, help requests, recommendations, and competitor negative reviews from multiple channels (Xiaohongshu, Douyin, Zhihu, Amazon, TikTok, etc.).
- **Tools**: MediaCrawler (Domestic), Bright Data (Overseas), Firecrawl (Web), OpenClaw (AI-assisted).
- **Organization**: De-duplication, de-noising, classification (content type, need type, user type, emotional intensity).

### 3. Trend & Intensity Analysis
- **Trends**: Calculate long-tail keyword growth rates to identify explosive (>100%) or fast-growing (30%-100%) needs.
- **Intensity Score**: Need Intensity = Frequency×0.3 + Emotion×0.2 + Growth×0.2 + Willingness to Pay×0.2 + Market Size×0.1.

### 4. Deep Needs Discovery
- **Three-Layer Discovery**: Identify what users say directly (Needs), hidden dissatisfaction (Pain Points), and unexpected surprises (Delight Points).
- **Theoretic Diagnosis**: Apply Maslow's Hierarchy, Two-Factor Theory, etc.
- **5 Whys**: Find root causes through continuous questioning and define core value.

### 5. Product Innovation Output
- **Functional Layering**:
    - Layers 1-2: Survival (Basic/Improved functions)
    - Layers 3-4: Profit (Deepened/Insightful functions)
    - Layers 5-6: Brand (Delightful/Disruptive functions)
- **Phased Strategy**: Plan pricing and marketing copy for MVP (Verify needs), Growth (Establish differentiation), and Maturity (Build barriers).
