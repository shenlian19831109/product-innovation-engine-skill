# Product Innovation Engine Skill

This repository contains the **Product Innovation Engine Skill**, designed to empower various AI assistants in extracting customer needs from specific scenarios and transforming them into high-value product insights. It provides a structured methodology, prompt library, and execution workflow for needs discovery and product innovation.

## Key Features:

- **Universal Applicability**: While optimized for Manus, the core methodologies and prompts are designed to be compatible with any advanced AI assistant (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude, DeepSeek) capable of understanding and executing structured instructions.
- **Comprehensive Workflow**: Guides AI assistants through a 7-step process: Scenario Segmentation, Data Collection & Organization, Trend & Intensity Analysis, Deep Needs Discovery, and Product Innovation Output.
- **Multi-language Support**: Includes both Chinese and English versions of the skill.

## How to Use with Your AI Assistant:

Simply provide your AI assistant with the content of the `SKILL.md` file (either Chinese or English version) and instruct it to follow the outlined methodology. The skill will guide the AI in performing market research, competitor analysis, product definition, and MVP planning based on user input.
