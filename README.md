A universal AI-powered engine for **needs discovery** and **product innovation**, designed to empower various AI assistants in extracting customer needs from specific scenarios and transforming them into high-value product insights. This repository provides a structured methodology, comprehensive prompt library, and execution workflow for **market research**, **competitor analysis**, **product definition**, and **MVP planning**.

## 🚀 Problem We Solve

In the fast-paced world of product development, identifying genuine user needs and translating them into innovative solutions is a constant challenge. Many product ideas fail due to a lack of deep understanding of user pain points, market trends, or a structured approach to innovation. Traditional methods are often time-consuming, prone to bias, and lack the scalability needed in today's dynamic environment.

## ✨ Our Solution: The Product Innovation Engine Skill

This AI-driven skill provides a robust, repeatable framework that guides AI assistants through a systematic process of uncovering, analyzing, and transforming raw user expressions into actionable product insights and innovative solutions. It moves beyond superficial analysis to delve into the core 
of user motivations, pain points, and unmet desires.

## 💡 Key Features & Benefits

- **Universal Applicability**: While optimized for Manus, the core methodologies and prompt engineering techniques are designed to be compatible with any advanced AI assistant (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude, DeepSeek) capable of understanding and executing structured instructions.
- **Comprehensive Workflow**: Guides AI assistants through a 7-step process:
    1.  **Scenario Segmentation**: Breaking down complex user contexts into manageable scenarios.
    2.  **Data Collection & Organization**: Systematically gathering and structuring diverse data sources.
    3.  **Trend & Intensity Analysis**: Identifying emerging patterns and the severity of user needs.
    4.  **Deep Needs Discovery (Pain Points & Delight Points)**: Utilizing techniques like 5 Whys to uncover root causes and core desires.
    5.  **Value Ladder Analysis**: Mapping discovered needs to different levels of user value.
    6.  **Product Innovation Output**: Generating concrete, actionable product ideas and feature sets.
    7.  **Transparency & Traceability (V9.2 Update)**: Ensures all conclusions are backed by verifiable data, including original source samples, data processing logs, and confidence scores.
- **Multi-language Support**: Includes both Chinese and English versions of the skill, making it accessible to a global audience.
- **Actionable Outputs**: Delivers structured reports, detailed innovation proposals, and clear next steps for MVP development.

## ⚡ Quick Start (for AI Assistants)

To leverage the Product Innovation Engine Skill, simply provide your AI assistant with the content of the `SKILL.md` file (either Chinese or English version) and instruct it to follow the outlined methodology. The skill will guide the AI in performing market research, competitor analysis, product definition, and MVP planning based on your input.

**Example Prompt for your AI Assistant:**

```
请使用 Product Innovation Engine Skill，针对“职场新人如何高效管理个人财务”这一场景，生成一份产品创新方案。
```

**Expected Output:**

Your AI assistant will then proceed through the 7-step workflow, asking clarifying questions and presenting intermediate findings, eventually delivering a comprehensive product innovation proposal, complete with identified pain points, proposed solutions, market analysis, and a confidence score for its recommendations.
